# AgentFarm One-Page Product Narrative

## What AgentFarm Is
AgentFarm is an AI workforce platform for engineering teams. It gives every company deployable AI teammates, starting with a Developer Agent, that can execute real work inside existing tools like GitHub, Jira, Teams, and company email.

## The Problem
Most AI tools stop at suggestions and chat. Engineering teams still carry the operational burden:
1. Moving work across systems manually
2. Waiting on approvals and handoffs
3. Lacking traceability for AI-generated actions
4. Struggling to trust autonomous execution in production workflows

Companies want AI that can do work, not just advise, but they need governance, safety, and auditability before they can trust it.

## The Solution
AgentFarm combines autonomy with control:
1. Role-based AI agents that perform engineering tasks end-to-end
2. Risk-aware action routing: low-risk actions can execute, medium and high-risk actions require human approval
3. Full audit and evidence trail for every important decision and action
4. Tenant-isolated runtime architecture on Azure for enterprise trust and security
5. Unified operational visibility through a real-time dashboard

## How It Works
1. A company signs up and provisions a tenant workspace
2. A Developer Agent runtime is deployed in an isolated environment
3. Connectors are enabled for GitHub, Jira, Teams, and company email
4. The agent receives tasks, classifies risk, and executes or requests approval
5. Every step is logged, observable, and reviewable in dashboard views

## Why This Matters
AgentFarm is designed for real enterprise constraints:
1. Autonomy without losing human control
2. Speed without sacrificing compliance
3. AI productivity with operational accountability

This creates a practical path from AI assistant to AI teammate.

## Who It Is For
1. Engineering leaders who want measurable AI execution, not just copilots
2. Security and compliance teams that require audit-ready control points
3. Platform and DevOps teams that need reliable, isolated, monitorable runtime behavior

## What Makes AgentFarm Different
1. Built for operational execution, not just conversation
2. Approval-driven safety model is part of the core workflow
3. Evidence and audit model is designed for release-gate and compliance reviews
4. Clear architecture split across control plane, runtime plane, and evidence plane

## Vision
Start with the Developer Agent, prove quality and trust, then expand to a full role-based AI team including QA, DevOps, and manager-facing roles under the same governance model.

## 30-Second Pitch
AgentFarm is the AI workforce platform for engineering teams. Instead of just chatting, our role-based AI agents execute real work across GitHub, Jira, Teams, and email. Every action is risk-classified, medium and high-risk changes require human approval, and the entire flow is auditable end-to-end. The result is autonomous execution with enterprise-grade control.
