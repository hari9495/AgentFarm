# AgentFarm Vision and Positioning
## Vision
Help companies scale output without scaling headcount by using role-based AI agents.
## Positioning
AgentFarm is not just a chatbot and not just automation scripts.
It is an AI workforce system with role-based agents.
## Target Users
1. CTOs
2. Engineering managers
3. Operations leaders
4. Growing teams with hiring pressure
## Core Promise
1. Onboard an AI agent quickly
2. Run the agent in real company workflows
3. Keep strong human control and auditability
4. Show measurable business value
## Why AgentFarm Wins
1. Human-like identity behavior in company tools
2. Role-based quality, not generic responses
3. Safe autonomy with mandatory approvals for risk
4. Clear value tracking and cost visibility
